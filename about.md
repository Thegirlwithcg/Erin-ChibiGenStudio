## Empowering the Next Generation of 3D Artists

At Chibi Gen Studio, we believe that creating adorable 3D characters shouldn't be a technical nightmare. Our mission is to bridge the gap between imagination and production.

Founded by **Erin n Patt**, a passionate character artist and developer, our studio specializes in creating intuitive Blender add-ons and high-quality assets. We understand the pain points of repetitive modeling tasks — rigging, retopology, and base mesh creation. That's why we built tools like **Chibi Gen Pro 2**: to handle the technical heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters most — the art.

Whether you are an indie game developer, a VTuber creator, or a 3D hobbyist, Chibi Gen Studio is here to support your journey with tools that work and courses that teach.

## Meet Erin n Patt

> "Hi, I'm Erin n Patt (Nichada Patthanawong), the lead artist and developer behind Chibi Gen Studio."

With a background in both technical art and character design, I realized that many artists struggle with the complex technicalities of 3D modeling. I wanted to change that. I combine my artistic skills with Python scripting to create Blender add-ons that solve real-world problems.

My goal is simple: to make professional-grade character creation accessible to everyone. When I'm not coding new features for Chibi Gen Pro, I'm creating in-depth tutorials to share my workflow secrets with the community.
